Project Overview

Lymm Fish Bar is a well-known local takeaway in Lymm village. The existing shopfront signage had become dated and wasn't doing the business justice — the branding was hard to read from the road, and the frontage didn't reflect the quality of what they were selling.

Digi Print was brought in to produce and install a new fascia sign, giving the shopfront a cleaner, bolder look that would stand out at street level and read clearly to passing trade and regular customers. We managed the full process — from sizing and artwork prep through to fabrication and on-site installation.

The job was completed in a single visit with minimal disruption during trading hours. The finished signage uses durable outdoor-grade materials built to last through a Manchester winter, with a clean print finish that holds its colour over time.

The Result

The new fascia is cleaner, more legible from distance, and immediately looks more professional — which matters when footfall is the lifeblood of a local food business. A straightforward shopfront signage job, done properly and to schedule.

Common Questions

Do you install shop signage as well as produce it?

Yes, supply and install is standard. We handle fabrication, delivery, and fitting. If you just need installation of an existing sign, we can quote for that too.

What types of shopfront signage do you produce?

Flat fascia signs, built-up lettering, tray signs, illuminated sign faces, foam-cut letters, and vinyl applied to existing shopfronts. What we recommend depends on your building, budget, and how you want it to look — we'll advise when you enquire.

How long does a shop sign take from enquiry to fitting?

Most standard fascia signs take 5–10 working days to produce once artwork is approved. If you have a specific opening date or deadline, tell us upfront and we'll work back from it.

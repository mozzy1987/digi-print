Project Overview

Lymm Fish Bar needed a shopfront signage refresh that would give the takeaway a cleaner, more visible frontage on a busy local high street.

Digi Print produced and installed fascia signage with a practical, durable finish, helping the business look more professional while staying clear and recognisable for regular customers and passing trade.

The Result

The finished shop signage gives Lymm Fish Bar a stronger street presence, sharper curb appeal, and a more polished first impression for customers arriving on foot or driving past.

Digi Print supports shop signage, fascia signs, external graphics, and signage installation projects across Manchester, Greater Manchester, Cheshire, and the North West.

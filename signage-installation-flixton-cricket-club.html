Project Overview

Flixton Cricket Club needed several signage updates completed around the ground ahead of the new season — a new sign for the storage shed, refreshed match and event banners, and a new sponsor board to give their sponsors proper visibility at the ground.

Digi Print produced all three elements in-house, working from the club's brief to get sizes, finishes, and fixings right for each location. The sponsor board was produced as a printed rigid panel with appropriate outdoor specification. The banners were produced in durable PVC with reinforced edges and eyelets for secure attachment around the ground perimeter.

Installation was completed on-site in a single day, fitted around the club's schedule to avoid disruption during use. Working at a live sports ground means being aware of access, ground conditions, and the need to leave everything clean and properly fixed — which we did.

The Result

The updated signs and banners made key areas around the ground look sharper and more organised. Sponsors have a clearer, more professional presence, and the overall presentation of the club is significantly improved — both for members and visiting teams.

Common Questions

Can you produce and install outdoor signage for sports clubs?

Yes. We've worked with clubs across Greater Manchester on everything from storage shed signs and banners to sponsor boards. We're used to working at live sites around fixtures and events.

Do you make sponsor boards for clubs?

Yes — printed sponsor panels and boards are a regular part of our work. We can produce them in standard sizes or custom dimensions to suit your ground layout.

What's the difference between a banner and a rigid sign?

Banners are typically PVC printed panels — lightweight, easy to put up and take down, good for event and sponsor use. Rigid signs (aluminium, foam PVC, dibond) are more permanent and weatherproof. We'll recommend the right option for where it's going and how long it needs to last.

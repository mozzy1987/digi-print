What We Offer

Shop Signage Services We Cover

From traditional painted fascias to modern illuminated channel letters — we offer the full range of shopfront signage solutions.

🏪

Fascia Signs

The backbone of your shopfront. We design and fabricate flat panel fascia signs, tray fascias, and projecting signs that carry your brand name and identity with clarity and authority.

🔤

Built-Up Lettering

Individually fabricated 3D letters create real depth and visual impact on any shopfront. Available in aluminium, acrylic, foam PVC, and brushed stainless steel to match your brand aesthetic.

💡

Illuminated Signs

LED-illuminated fascias, lightboxes, and backlit channel letters keep your brand visible day and night. Energy-efficient, low-maintenance, and highly impactful on busy Manchester streets.

📐

Tray Signs & Trims

Aluminium tray fascias with decorative trims give a polished, professional finish that suits both modern retail units and traditional premises. Fully customisable in any RAL colour.

🪧

Post-Mounted Signs

Freestanding post signs and monoliths are ideal for out-of-town retail parks, forecourts, and commercial premises where visibility from the road is key.

✨

Halo-Lit Letters

Reverse-lit or halo-illuminated letters cast a distinctive glow behind each character — creating a premium, upmarket look that elevates your brand perception instantly.

Signage and Graphics That Set the Scene for Your Venue

In the hospitality sector, atmosphere is everything. The right signage and interior graphics don't just communicate — they contribute to the experience your customers have from the moment they arrive. Digi Print works with restaurants, bars, pubs, hotels, cafés, takeaways and event venues across Greater Manchester to deliver signage that's on-brand, durable and beautifully executed.

From hand-crafted-looking exterior signs and illuminated fascias to bold wall murals, decorative film and bespoke menu board graphics — we supply and install everything. We serve venues in Manchester city centre, the Northern Quarter, Ancoats, Salford, Trafford and across the wider North West.

  • Exterior signs, fascias and illuminated venue signage
  • Window graphics, frosted film and decorative glass film
  • Interior wall murals and feature wall graphics
  • Menu boards, A-boards and chalk-effect vinyls
  • Architectural film — wood, marble and textured surface wraps
  • Wayfinding for multi-floor venues and hotel properties

What We Supply & Install

Hospitality Signage Solutions for Every Venue Type

Whether you're opening a new restaurant, rebranding a bar or refreshing a hotel interior — we have the products and expertise to deliver.

🍽️

Exterior Venue Signage

Fascia signs, illuminated letters, projecting signs and entrance graphics that draw customers in. Designed to reflect your venue's personality — from sleek and contemporary to rustic and characterful.

🖼️

Interior Wall Murals & Graphics

Large-scale wall graphics, photographic murals, branded feature walls and typographic installations. Transform a blank wall into a talking point and Instagram moment that keeps customers coming back.

🪟

Window Graphics & Decorative Film

Frosted film, patterned glass film, promotional graphics and privacy film for windows, partitions and glazed screens. Add atmosphere and branding to your venue's glass surfaces.

📋

Menu Boards & A-Boards

Printed menu inserts, chalk-effect vinyls, lightbox inserts and A-board graphics. Keep your offers, specials and promotions visible and on-brand — updated quickly when needed.

🏗️

Architectural & Surface Film

Wood-effect, marble and textured surface film applied to bar fronts, furniture, wall panels and joinery. Achieve a high-end finish at a fraction of the cost of replacement or traditional materials.

🗺️

Wayfinding & Room Signage

Directional signs, room name plates, toilet signs and floor directories for hotels, large restaurants and multi-floor venues. Consistent, branded and professionally installed.

How We Work

Hospitality Signage Delivered to Your Opening Date
